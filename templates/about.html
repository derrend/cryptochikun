Welcome to cryptochikun 2.0!

Worlds first battle betting crypto game based on pure risk aversion.

How does it work?

Inputs with > minimum required confirmations and an amount >= ((dust_threshold / 2) * 100) + network_transaction_fee are matched at random each time a new block is discovered.
The value of both inputs are sent to the return address of whichever input has the highest value.
Matching addresses/amounts are discarded and redrawn.
Deposit addresses can be reused.
